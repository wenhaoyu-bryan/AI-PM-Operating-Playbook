# AI PRD Lite Template

A lightweight PRD template for PM-led AI prototypes. Use this when you need to scope a prototype quickly — not a full product spec, but enough structure to direct AI-assisted development effectively.

---

## Problem

What problem does this solve? One or two sentences. Be specific about the workflow or decision that is currently broken.

## User

Who is the operator at the keyboard? What is their role, skill level, and current workflow?

## Goal

What does success look like? Define the outcome, not the feature.

## Non-goals

What is deliberately out of scope? Naming non-goals prevents scope creep and keeps the prototype focused.

## Inputs

What data, documents, or user actions does the system receive? Specify format and source.

## Outputs

What does the system produce? Artifacts, decisions, recommendations, actions.

## AI Capability

What specific AI capability is used at each step? (extraction, classification, reasoning, generation, retrieval, planning) Where does AI add value vs. where would rules suffice?

## Workflow

Describe the end-to-end flow. What triggers it? What are the steps? What is the final output or action?

## Human Review

Where does a human check, approve, or override the AI? What information does the reviewer need? What is the cost of getting it wrong?

## Evaluation

How do you measure whether this works? Quality metrics and workflow efficiency metrics. What is the baseline before AI?

## Failure Modes

What happens when the AI is wrong? What happens when the data is bad? What is the fallback?

## Prototype Scope

What is the minimum viable prototype? What data subset? What can be mocked? What must be real to validate the concept?

## Open Questions

What are you still unsure about? What assumptions need validation?
