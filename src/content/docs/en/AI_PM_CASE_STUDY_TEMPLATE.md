# AI PM Case Study Template

Use this template to document AI PM projects consistently. Fill in each section after completing a project or prototype. Keep it grounded — describe what actually happened, not what you wish had happened.

---

## 1. Context

What was the setting? Company, team, timeline, constraints.

## 2. User / Business Problem

What problem were users or the business facing? Be specific about the workflow that was broken, slow, or expensive.

## 3. Existing Workflow

How was the problem handled before AI? What tools, processes, or manual steps were involved?

## 4. AI Opportunity

Where in the workflow does AI add value? What specific capability (extraction, classification, reasoning, generation, retrieval) is relevant?

## 5. Data / Knowledge Inputs

What data or domain knowledge does the system need? What is the format, quality, and availability?

## 6. Product Concept

What is the one-sentence value proposition? What does the product do for the user?

## 7. Agent / AI Workflow

Describe the end-to-end workflow. What triggers it? What steps does the AI perform? What is the output?

## 8. Human-in-the-Loop Design

Where does a human review, approve, or override the AI? What does the reviewer need to see? What is the cost of a wrong AI decision at each point?

## 9. Evaluation Metrics

How do you measure success? Both output quality (correctness, precision) and workflow efficiency (time saved, steps reduced).

## 10. Prototype Scope

What was included in the prototype? What was deliberately excluded? What was mocked vs. real?

## 11. Risks and Constraints

What are the production risks? Data privacy, model reliability, cost, latency, user trust, compliance?

## 12. Demo / Portfolio Narrative

How do you explain this project to someone reviewing your work? What is the before/after story? What is the demo moment?
