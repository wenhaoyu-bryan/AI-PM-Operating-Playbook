# Product Thinking

This section covers how the AI PM role differs from traditional product management — what changes, what stays the same, and what new competencies matter.

## Contents

- [AI PM Role](ai-pm-role.md) — Evolution of the PM role in AI-native product development, core competencies, growth path, and workflow changes

## When to use this

- When explaining the AI PM role to someone unfamiliar with it
- When building your own AI PM competency framework
- When thinking about how AI changes product workflows

## Connection to AI PM work

Product thinking is the foundation. Before designing agents, writing prompts, or building prototypes, an AI PM needs a clear model of how AI changes the product manager's responsibilities, decision-making, and delivery process.
